Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026.

Retire & Roam ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect, why, and what choices you have. If anything is unclear, write to Retiretravel0034@gmail.com and a real person will reply.

1. What we collect

  • Analytics data — pages viewed, approximate location (city level), device type, referring site. Collected via Google Analytics (GA4, ID: G-4J046JXWVC).
  • Advertising data — if Google AdSense or similar networks serve ads on our pages, they may use cookies and the Google Advertising ID to show relevant ads.
  • Information you give us — your name, email, and message when you use the contact form or subscribe to our newsletter.

2. How we use it

  • To understand which stories readers enjoy, so we write more of them.
  • To reply to your messages and send our twice-monthly newsletter (only if you opt in).
  • To support the site through advertising. We do not sell personal information.

3. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies for analytics, ad personalization, and remembering your preferences (for example, dismissing the welcome card). You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. The site continues to work either way.

4. Google AdSense & third-party vendors

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may opt out of some third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized ads at aboutads.info.

5. Children

This site is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Your rights

You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing Retiretravel0034@gmail.com. Residents of California (CCPA) and the EU/UK (GDPR) have additional rights, including the right to opt out of the "sale" of personal information — we do not sell personal information.

7. Data retention & security

We keep newsletter contacts until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained per Google's default settings. We use reputable third-party services and reasonable safeguards to keep data secure, but no system is perfect.

8. Updates to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top and, when appropriate, notify newsletter subscribers.
